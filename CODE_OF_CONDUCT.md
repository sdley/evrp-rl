# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in this academic and research project a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone. We pledge to act with respect, consideration, and professionalism to foster an open and collaborative community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and project.
- Showing empathy towards other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form.
- Disruptive or exclusionary behavior that inhibits collaboration.
- Personal attacks or insults.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers and project leaders will enforce this Code of Conduct fairly and consistently. Consequences of unacceptable behavior may include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the project.
